About Us



Welcome to Oxford Primary School-Mbale

Oxford Primary School, is a private mixed Nursery and primary school located in Industrial Division Mbale City Uganda, its a day and boarding school providing a local curriculum. Oxford Primary school is doing all it takes to measure to the international standards. It's situated along Ndiya road-Maluku and has an enrolement of over 750 pupils with 38 well trained teachers and over 35 non-teaching staff members. The pupils come from all over the country and some other parts of the world. The school has been and will continue to be one of the academic hubs of Uganda because of the balanced curriculum offered. We are multi cultural institution that provides a well-rounded balanced education to childeren from different backgrounds.


Mission and Anthem

The school mission is to provide education that will develop an all-rounded person who is ready to face the challenges of the world. The schoul anthem clearly indicates that children, teachers and parents of Oxford School glorify the lord and their prayers ascend on high to achieve school goals and ampirations.

The academic performance is to the required standard and this is because of the well-trained staff members who are talented in handling pupils. Discipline is a vital element in grooming pupils. It Is the key to ssuccess as evidenced by the good performance for many consecutive years
